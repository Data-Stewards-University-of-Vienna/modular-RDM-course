## Ethics and Legal

### Objectives
- Understand key ethics and legal requirements for research at the University of Vienna.
- Explain why CARE principles and community-centered approaches matter in research data management.
- Distinguish between common licensing options for data, code, hardware, and machine learning outputs.
- Identify core legal frameworks relevant to research, including GDPR, copyright, and intellectual property.
- Recognize when additional legal or ethical obligations apply, such as human research, animal research, and the Nagoya Protocol.
- Determine when informed consent is required and what should be included in consent documentation.

## Conclusion
- Ethical and legal considerations are core parts of responsible research data management, not optional add-ons.
- Choosing suitable licenses, protecting personal and sensitive data, and documenting informed consent improve transparency, reuse, and compliance.
- Early consultation with relevant university support structures helps address complex cases such as human and animal research, indigenous data, and intellectual property.
